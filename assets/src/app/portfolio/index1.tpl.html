Absolute Yachts North America


Absolute is an Italian yacht brand based in Piacenza, Italy, manufacturing yachts ranging from 40 to 72 feet. Communicating the brand's focus on design, efficiency, performance and serviceability are a high priority. NOVO is responsible for Absolute North America's online marketing, including web design and development, email marketing, and social media marketing. The web site includes a home page, about page, dealer map, model information and galleries, and contact form.
